PICTURES OF CRADLESTONE MALL

CRADLESTONE MALL is perfectly located in the heart of MOGALE CITY in Johannesburg's West Rand. Named for its close proximity to the World Heritage Site,CRADLESTONE Mall is both an architectural masterpiece and is optimally located at the intersection of the N14/R28 highways and Hendrick Potgieter and conveniently adjecent to the Pinehaven Interchange. CRADLESTONE Mall offers the perfect mix of 160 quality shops, restuarants and services-all in a fresh and vibrant atmoshere. In addition to the nearly 4300 convenient parking bays, specialised facilities for the disabled and family parking, the mall also makes use of the very latest energy saving technologies, reducing the Mall's carbon footprint and ensuring that Cradlestone Mall is an exceptional and unforgettable shopping experience. CRADLESTONE Mall is proudly owned and developed by Sasol Pension Fund, Redefine and Dorpstraat.
